User Registration

Please register your details for Indian Railways Unreserved Ticketing. This is one time activity and after successful registration, you can login into UTS Mobile Ticketing Website as well as UTSonMobile application on Android, Windows & iOS based mobile phones with your Mobile Number as Login ID and choosen Password.

After successful registration, a wallet will be created with zero balance, called as Railway-Wallet (R-WALLET). User can Recharge R-WALLET through UTS Mobile Ticketing Website or UTSonMobile application and he/she can Book Unreserved Tickets (Journey, Platform and Season) through UTSonMobile application after making payment through R-WALLET or OTHERS payment mode options.
