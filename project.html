Process flow for online recharge of ATVM Smart Card

1. Select the menu option "SMART CARD RECHARGE" after successful login into the portal.

2. Feed/Enter the smart card number, which is printed on the physical card.

3. Enter the valid recharge amount and agree to complete the recharge at ATVM within specified time period.

4. Complete the payment using any available payment modes viz.NetBanking, Debit Card and UPI.

5. After successful of the payment, visit any ATVM within Railway zone to complete the recharge.
